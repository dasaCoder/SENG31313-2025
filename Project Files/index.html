Fuel Pass

FuelPass is a fuel management system modeled after a real-world solution from our country’s economic crisis. It enables vehicle owners to register for weekly fuel quotas via QR codes, scanned at stations to track usage. With driver and station portals, live SMS alerts, and an admin panel, it ensures transparent and efficient fuel distribution.
